WELCOME !

Since its takeover in 2022, our establishment has been infused with a passion for authentic cuisine and the art of hospitality, creating a warm and friendly atmosphere for each of our visitors.

A FAMILY BUSINESS...

With a wealth of experience in the field of catering, our eldest brother took over the reins of this iconic place in Nyons.

Every morning, our father welcomes you with a smile to serve you the best coffee in town. Joined by our mother as lunch approaches, the kitchen comes to life to prepare simple and delicious meals, highlighting local flavors.

Throughout the day, our three brothers ensure that your experience is exceptional. We offer a diverse selection of delights : carefully crafted cocktails, thoughtfully chosen wines, and of course, flavorful coffees.

Our menu showcases our specialties of "ravioles", a French regional speciality consisting of two layers of pasta surrounding a filling of French cheese, as well as charcuterie platters, bagels, croque-monsieurs, and fresh salads, all prepared with local and seasonal ingredients.

Whether you prefer to enjoy the warm atmosphere of our indoor dining area or the charm of our lovely terrace, we strive to make your time with us an unforgettable experience.

We look forward to welcoming you and sharing our love for food and camaraderie with you.
